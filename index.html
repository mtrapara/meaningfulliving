About

Our company stands by the pillars of establishing and maintaining a healthy and safe environment for our clients. We believe there is no cookie-cutter solution in terms of home care. We tailor our services to meet each clients’ specific needs. Our services include, but are not limited to: meal preparation grocery shopping monitoring medication home cleaning palliative care accompaniment to doctor’s appointments, dinners, events, etc. We can also arrange for physiotherapist, massage therapist, and hygienist visits to clients’ homes.
